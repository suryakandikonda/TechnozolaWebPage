The Technozola association is a student association started in the year 2017.Technozola aims to enhance the innovative ideas within the technozola community.Technozola aims at conducting workshops,events and seminars which are beneficial to the students.Its purpose is to create a medium for the students for expressing their innovative skills.The Technozola team is involved in developing and monitoring professional educational programs and updating the skills of students as per trends in the technology
